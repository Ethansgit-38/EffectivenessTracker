Type your numbers straight into the table — everything recalculates live and saves automatically in this browser. Daily tracks one row per day; Monthly lets you drop in whole-month totals (this month, last month) to see the bigger arc. Calls Taken are appointments sat on your calendar; Calls Made is outbound dials — the calls you make in a no-show slot instead of wasting it. The Effectiveness Score reads how much you closed relative to the appointments and hours it took — closing more with less scores higher. Use Export backup to save a copy or hand a client their own file.
