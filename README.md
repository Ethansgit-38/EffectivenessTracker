# Effectiveness Tracker

A free, no-login productivity + effectiveness tracker for sales reps. One page.
Type in your numbers, see how effective you actually are — not just how busy.

**Use it live:** _(link goes here once hosting is on)_

Or download `index.html` and double-click it — works offline, nothing to install.

## What it tracks
Hours worked · Calls Taken (appointments sat) · Calls Made (dials) · Deals Closed · Revenue.

It auto-calculates your **Close %**, **Deals/hr**, **Rev/Call**, and a 0–100
**Effectiveness Score** — how much you closed relative to the effort it took. Closing
more with fewer calls and hours scores higher.

- **Daily or Monthly** view — track day-by-day or drop in whole-month totals.
- **Scores on whatever you have** — even just calls + cash gives you a read.
- **Industry presets** — High-Ticket Sales, Door-to-Door, Real Estate, Insurance, Generic.
- Saves in your browser. Export/Import to back up or move machines.
